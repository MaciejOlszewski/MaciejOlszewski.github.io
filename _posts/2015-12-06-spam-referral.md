I'm sure you are one of those people dealing with huge amount of spam referral.

![Spam referral example](/images/post_imgs/spam2.png)

If so, you should definitely check [piwik](https://github.com/piwik/referrer-spam-blacklist) repository for community driven list of spam referral URLs.
You can read about spam referral on [PIWIK blog site](https://piwik.org/blog/2015/05/stopping-referrer-spam/) and his Github repository.

For extra information as well as information how to setup Google Analytics filters read these two pages:

- [www.optimizesmart.com](https://www.optimizesmart.com/geek-guide-removing-referrer-spam-google-analytics/)
- [moz.com](https://moz.com/blog/how-to-stop-spam-bots-from-ruining-your-analytics-referral-data)


Thanks to piwik list we can easily filter spammers.
I *encourage* you to contribute to this nice project!
